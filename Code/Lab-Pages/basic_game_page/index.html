Dishonored

In Dishonored you play as Corvo Attano, former royal bodyguard and framed for the murder of the Empress. You join the Loyalists to exact your revenge and find the missing Empress' daughter. Play the game however you want with various combinations of weapons, tools and supernatural powers. Stick to the shadows or charge in ruthlessly, you have multiple ways to dispatch your enemies. Each level has a unique layout to explore different ways to reach your goal. The game adapts to your playstyle, eventually determining the outcome of the game.

"Dishonored's engrossing world and intoxicating interplay of supernatural powers make it a game you'll want to play more than once." - Chris Waters, Gamespot
